What Makes Opinion Quizzes Different?

Traditional quizzes test what you know. Opinion quizzes on Quiz Dangal test what you think — and whether your perspective aligns with the majority. There are no objectively “correct” answers. Instead, each question presents a trending topic, a cultural debate, or a real-time poll, and you vote for the option that resonates with you. After the round closes, we reveal the full percentage breakdown so you can see exactly how India voted. This format eliminates performance anxiety and makes every quiz feel social, fun, and insightful.

How Scoring Works in Opinion Quizzes

Since there is no “right” answer, opinion quiz scoring is based on majority alignment. If your answer matches the majority vote, you get +1 point. If you are in the minority, you score 0 for that question (no negative marking). The final leaderboard is ranked by total points, and tie-breakers go to the player who submitted their last correct (majority) answer the fastest. This system rewards both social intuition and speed, creating an engaging competitive dynamic that keeps players coming back.
